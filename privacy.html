Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

At Kiddo Puzzle, we believe that early childhood play should be secure, uninterrupted, and educational. We are dedicated to protecting the privacy of young players and their families. This privacy policy describes the information we collect and how we handle it.

2. COPPA Compliance (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Our app is designed strictly for kids aged 2–7. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from children.

3. Zero Third-Party Advertising & Tracking

Unlike many modern games, Kiddo Puzzle contains **no third-party advertisements or trackers**. There are no banner ads, pop-ups, or analytics APIs that send child behavior profiles to external ad networks.

4. Information We Collect

We do not require account creation, log-ins, or profile building. The app runs completely locally on your device. We only receive:

  • Non-Personal Device Data: Simple device type (e.g., iPad vs. iPhone) and system versions to debug rendering issues. This is never linked to a real name or user identity.
  • Purchase Information: We support one-time purchases to unlock premium puzzles. This transaction is handled securely by Apple StoreKit; we never see or store your payment details.

5. Parental Gate Restrictions

Any external interactions (such as restoring purchases or accessing settings) are guarded by a math-verification parental gate. This prevents kids from making accidental modifications or viewing external menus.
