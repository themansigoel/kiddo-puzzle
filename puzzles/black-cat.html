Why the Black Cat Puzzle is Great for Learning:

  • Silhouette Contour Tracking: Kids drag and align the Black Cat into its shaded outline, training focus and hand-eye coordinates.

  • Positive Feedback loop: Fun celebratory balloon-popping animations reward correct matches, strengthening childhood confidence.
